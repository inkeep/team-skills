### Unique Conversion Tactics

1. **Interactive conversation carousel** - Product demo in hero without form

### Homepage Analysis

**Hero:**
- **Headline:** "Fin. The #1 AI Agent for customer service"
- **Subheadline:** "The highest performing AI Agent"
- **Secondary:** "The first AI agent that delivers human-quality customer service"

**Key Differentiators in Hero:**
- "Fin AI Engine™" - patented architecture
- "Set up in under an hour"
- "67% average resolution rate, improving 1% every month"
- Handles "complex queries, not just FAQs"

**CTAs (Multiple tiers):**
1. Primary: "Start free trial"
2. Secondary: "Get a demo"
3. Tertiary: "View demo"
4. Alternative: "Contact sales"

**Social Proof:**
- G2 Badge: "#1 AGENT ON G2" (prominent)
- 20+ enterprise logos: Anthropic, Snowflake, Crypto.com, Mercury, Linear, Asana, Miro, Toast, DoorDash, Riot Games, Vanta

**Featured Testimonial:**
> "Fin is in a completely different league. It's now involved in 99% of conversations and successfully resolves up to 65% end-to-end—even the more complex ones."
> — Angelo Livanos, VP of Global Support, Lightspeed

### Trial & Demo Flow

**Trial Approach:**
- 14-day free trial
- No credit card required
- Unlimited usage during trial
- Full feature access

**Demo Page (/view-demos):**
- Interactive video demos (6m36s Wistia videos)
- Multiple use-case demonstrations
- Form fields: Name, Email, Company size, Marketing opt-in

### ROI Calculator (High-Intent Tool)

**URL:** fin.ai/roi-calculator

**Input Variables:**
- Conversations per month (slider)
- Number of support employees
- Annual cost per employee
- Resolution rate (with note: "Most customers see 67% rate, improving 1% monthly")

**Output Metrics:**
- 3-year total savings
- ROI percentage
- Year-by-year breakdown
- Agents freed up

**Example Output:** $6.4M savings over 3 years, 1,163% ROI

**Trust Signal:** "FIN MILLION DOLLAR GUARANTEE"

### Pricing Model

- **$0.99 per resolution** (outcome-based)
- Minimum commitments apply
- No platform fee standalone
- $29/helpdesk seat if using Intercom

### Case Study Metrics

| Company | Key Metric | Result |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Lightspeed | Fin involvement | 99% |
| Lightspeed | Resolution rate | 65% |
| Databox | Resolution growth | 30% → 55% |
| Databox | Revenue increase | 40% |

| Anthropic | Hours saved | 1,700+ (first month) |
| Synthesia | Hours saved | 1,300+ (6 months) |

**Aggregate Metrics:**
- 40+ million resolved conversations
- 67% average resolution rate
- 45 languages supported

### Unique Conversion Tactics

1. **ROI Calculator** - Personalized savings with company inputs
2. **Free trial (no credit card)** - Lowest friction entry
3. **Outcome-based pricing ($0.99/resolution)** - Removes ROI objection
4. **G2 #1 badge prominent** - Third-party validation
5. **Competitive comparison page** - Direct Fin vs Zendesk
6. **Million Dollar Guarantee** - Risk reversal
7. **Video demos gated** - Lead capture with value exchange

## Implications for Inkeep

### What to Adopt

1. **From Glean:**
   - Quantified time/productivity metrics prominently displayed
   - Multiple analyst recognition badges (G2, Gartner)
   - Executive testimonials with titles and companies

2. **From Sierra:**
   - Interactive product demo in hero (conversation carousel)
   - Narrative case study headlines ("How X transformed Y")
   - Outcomes-based pricing messaging
   - Soft CTA language ("Learn more" reduces commitment anxiety)

3. **From Fin AI:**
   - ROI calculator for personalized value demonstration
   - Free trial option for low-friction entry
   - Competitive comparison page (Fin vs Zendesk pattern)
   - G2 #1 badge if applicable

### Recommended Priority Actions

| Priority | Action | Based On |
