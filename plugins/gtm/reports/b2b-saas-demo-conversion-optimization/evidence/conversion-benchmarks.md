## Findings

### Finding: Inkeep's demo page conversion is below median
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Benchmark comparison

| Stage | Industry Median | Top Performers | Inkeep Current |
|-------|-----------------|----------------|----------------|
| Landing page → form submission | 3.8% | 8-12% | 3.79% |
| Homepage → demo click | 2-3% | 5-8% | 0.20% |
| Blog → demo | 0.5-1% | 2-3% | ~0% |
| Demo form → booked meeting | 62% | 70%+ | N/A |

**Implications:** Inkeep's demo page conversion is at median, but homepage and blog conversion are significantly below.

### Finding: Top performers achieve 8-12% demo request conversion
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Multiple benchmark reports

- Median SaaS landing page: 3.8%
- Top performers: 8-12%
- Best-in-class: 15-25%
- "Significant performance gap between companies using optimization best practices"

**Implications:** There's substantial upside if Inkeep implements best practices.

### Finding: Embedded calendars improve form-to-meeting by 3.75%
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Chili Piper data

- With live call option: 69.2% form-to-meeting
- Without: 66.7% form-to-meeting
- Small improvement with large volume = significant impact

**Implications:** Adding embedded calendar is low-effort, measurable improvement.

### Finding: AI-driven personalization adds ~10% conversion uplift
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** 2025-2026 research

- "10% uplift in conversions when teams use AI-driven personalization"
- Applies to content, outreach, and in-app experiences
- Personalized CTAs outperform default by 202%

**Implications:** Future optimization could include dynamic content based on visitor segment.
