## Findings

### Finding: Above-fold CTAs are critical - 57% of time spent there
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Industry research

- "57% of viewing time spent on the first screenful"
- "Above-fold content carries disproportionate weight"
- Skipping above-fold CTA is a "common mistake"
- Most common CTA spots: sticky header + hero section

**Implications:** Inkeep homepage hero must have clear, prominent demo CTA.

### Finding: Urgent, specific CTAs outperform generic by 200%+
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** SaaS Hero, multiple studies

- "Book Demo in 30 Seconds" delivers 200%+ higher conversions than "Learn More"
- Action-oriented language outperforms passive
- Specificity reduces friction ("30-minute call" vs "Contact us")

**Implications:** "Get a Demo" could become "Book a 15-min Demo" or similar specific language.

### Finding: Dual CTA strategy (trial + demo) captures different intents
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Datadog, Supabase, Segment analysis

**Datadog pattern:**
- Primary: "Free trial" (self-serve)
- Secondary: "Request a personalized demo" (sales-assisted)
- Two-tier strategy targets different buyer maturity

**Supabase pattern:**
- Primary: "Start your project" (developer-first, repeated)
- Secondary: "Request a demo" (enterprise, less prominent)
- Self-service dominates; demo for complex needs

**Segment pattern:**
- Primary: "Start for free" (freemium)
- Secondary: "Contact sales" (serves as demo pathway)
- No dedicated demo button - sales engagement for complex needs

**Implications:** Inkeep should consider whether self-serve option makes sense alongside demo.

### Finding: Single-goal pages convert 28% better
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Unbounce research

- Single CTA pages: 13.5% conversion
- Multiple CTAs: 10.5% conversion
- Decision paralysis reduces conversion
- "Every call to action should support that singular goal"

**Implications:** If demo is the goal, minimize competing CTAs on homepage.

### Finding: Product screenshots + trust signals increase demo rates 25%
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Multiple sources

- "Actual product interface screenshots demonstrating value"
- G2 badges, analyst recognition near CTA
- Customer logos establish credibility
- Datadog shows Gartner Magic Quadrant Leader badges

**Implications:** Inkeep homepage should show product UI and G2/analyst badges near CTA.
