## Findings

### Finding: Interactive demos drive 7.9x higher conversion than no demo
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** RevenueHero research

> "Demo engagement vs no demo = 7.9x improvement (24.35% vs 3.05%)"
> "Days to form submission: 8.3 days (no demo) → 6.8 days (demo) — 18% faster"

**Implications:** Adding interactive demo is highest-leverage conversion tactic available.

---

### Finding: Ungated demos outperform gated demos 2:1
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Chameleon/Navattic research

| Metric | Ungated | Gated |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Engagement rate | 19.40% | 9.20% |
| Impression rate | 7x higher | Baseline |
| % of top demos | 66% | 34% |
| Completion rate | 7% higher | - |

> "75% of top demos that gate collect only ONE field"
> "45% place lead capture at END of demo (not beginning)"

**Implications:** Inkeep should use ungated demos on homepage/product pages, gate only after step 5+ if at all.

---

### Finding: Optimal demo length is 5-12 steps with 5-second opening hook
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Navattic State of Interactive Demo 2025

> "Top 1% of demos stay within 5-12 steps"
> "First 2 steps critical for 'aha moment'"
> "5-second rule: State outcome immediately or majority drop off"

**Implications:** Inkeep demo should show ticket triage or deflection within first 2 steps.

---

### Finding: Multi-flow demos achieve 48% higher completion rates
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Navattic research

> "Multi-flow design: 48% higher completion rates than single-flow demos"

**Implications:** Create persona-specific demo paths (Support Manager vs Agent vs IT Buyer).

## AI Support Product Demo Best Practices

### What to Show in First 2 Steps (Aha Moment)
1. Incoming support ticket volume problem (visual)
2. AI instantly triages/categorizes → shows immediate value

### Core Demo Flow (8-10 steps)
1. Support queue overwhelmed (problem)
2. AI auto-categorizes ticket (aha moment)
3. Agent sees prioritized queue
4. Real-time response suggestions appear
5. Agent sends AI-assisted response
6. Complex ticket escalates gracefully
7. Customer satisfaction tracked
8. Metrics dashboard shows improvement
9. Integration with existing tools
10. CTA: Start trial / Book demo

### Persona-Specific Variations

| Persona | Focus | Key Metric to Show |
|---------|-------|-------------------|
| Support Manager | Cost savings, CSAT | 50% FCR improvement |
| Support Agent | Time savings, ease | 5 min/ticket reduction |
| IT Buyer | Integration, security | Uptime, compliance |
| Executive | ROI, headcount | Cost per ticket |

### Dynamic Content Opportunities
- Show industry-specific sample tickets
- Display projected metrics based on company size
- Highlight relevant integrations (Zendesk vs Intercom vs Freshdesk)
- Adapt compliance messaging (HIPAA for healthcare, SOC 2 for enterprise)

## Video Specifications by Page

### Homepage Hero
- **Length:** 60-90 seconds
- **Format:** Autoplay muted with captions
- **Content:** Problem → AI solution → result
- **Hosting:** Wistia with lazy loading
- **Expected lift:** 3-12% vs static

### Demo Page
- **Length:** 2-3 minutes
- **Format:** Click-to-play with captions
- **Content:** Full product walkthrough
- **Hosting:** Wistia with email capture CTA
- **Expected conversion:** 5-15%

### Customer Testimonial
- **Length:** 2-5 minutes
- **Format:** Interview style
- **Content:** Problem → Implementation → Results with metrics
- **Hosting:** Wistia + YouTube for reach

### Social/Ads
- **Length:** 15-30 seconds
- **Format:** Autoplay muted, vertical for mobile
- **Captions:** Mandatory (40% more views)
