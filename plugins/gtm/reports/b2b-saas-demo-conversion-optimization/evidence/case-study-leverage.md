## Findings

### Finding: Case studies are bottom-of-funnel content with high conversion
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** UXCam, industry research

- "If someone is reading a case study, they're genuinely interested"
- Readers want to see: similar company, genuine results, achievable outcomes
- Case studies = high-intent traffic
- Inkeep data confirms: 24.32% conversion rate

**Implications:** Case studies are Inkeep's highest-converting asset but receive minimal traffic.

### Finding: Strategic CTA placement in case studies maximizes conversion
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** ProofMap, Social Sellinator

- "Companies prioritizing strategic CTA placement—'Request a Demo' or 'See Pricing' at key moments"
- CTAs should appear at decision points within the story
- Challenge-Solution-Impact framework is "gold standard"

**Implications:** Inkeep case studies should have CTAs at solution reveal and results sections.

### Finding: Industry-specific organization doubles conversion
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** ProofMap research

- "One SaaS company doubled conversion rate from healthcare prospects after reorganizing case studies into industry-specific collections"
- "Smart companies create comprehensive libraries organized by sector"
- Allows prospects to find relevant stories easily
- Signals "specialized knowledge of their unique challenges"

**Implications:** Inkeep should organize case studies by vertical (DevTools, FinTech, etc.) and link from relevant use-case pages.

### Finding: Case studies need prominent placement across site
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Multiple sources

Current problem: Case studies convert at 24% but only 37 users reached them before /demo.

Placement strategies that work:
- Feature on homepage hero
- Link in blog post footers
- Embed in use-case pages
- Navigation menu visibility
- Related case study in sidebar

**Implications:** Every high-traffic page should link to relevant case study.

### Finding: Quantifiable metrics are essential
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Industry consensus

- "Quality trumps quantity"
- "Case studies with clear, compelling metrics perform best"
- Examples: revenue growth, operational efficiencies, cost savings
- Specific numbers outperform vague claims

**Implications:** Ensure Inkeep case studies lead with metrics (e.g., "50% ticket reduction").

## Placement Priority Matrix

| Page Type | Case Study Integration |
|-----------|------------------------|
| Homepage | Featured case study section with 2-3 logos + key metric |
| Blog posts | Footer link to most relevant case study |
| Use-case pages | Inline quote + metric + link to full case study |
| /demo page | 1-2 testimonial quotes with metrics |
| Tag pages | Related case studies by topic |
