## Findings

### Finding: Form field reduction dramatically improves conversion
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Multiple sources

- Reducing form fields from 10 to 6 = 15.65% increase in completions
- Reducing from 4 to 3 fields = 50% increase in conversions
- Chili Piper uses only 3 form fields
- Databox uses single-field (email only) approach

**Implications:** Inkeep's demo form should be audited for field count. Every non-essential field reduces conversion.

### Finding: Embedded calendars outperform traditional forms
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Chili Piper, Digioh research

- Only 4% of demo pages feature calendar schedulers above the fold
- Only 18% of B2B SaaS companies use scheduling tools over forms
- Customers with live call options have 69.2% form-to-meeting rate vs 66.7% average
- Embedding calendars eliminates navigation friction

**Implications:** Adding embedded Calendly/HubSpot scheduler could significantly improve demo completion.

### Finding: Social proof placement near CTA increases conversion 84-270%
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Unbounce, multiple studies

- Testimonials near CTAs/signup forms = 84-270% conversion lift
- Impact decreases significantly when buried in footers
- G2 badges and analyst recognition increase demo rates by 25%

**Implications:** Inkeep demo page needs testimonials and trust signals immediately adjacent to the form.

### Finding: "What to expect" framing reduces friction
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Powered by Search analysis

- Top demo pages explain "what you can expect from the demo"
- Datadog uses: "personalized demo with a Datadog engineer"
- Setting expectations (e.g., "30-minute call, see it live") reduces commitment anxiety

**Implications:** Inkeep demo page should add expectation-setting copy.

### Finding: Progressive/stepped forms reduce cognitive load
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Zendesk example

- Zendesk steps users "through questions one by one"
- Reduces perceived form length
- Works better for complex qualification

**Implications:** Consider multi-step form if qualification is needed.
