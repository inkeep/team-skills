**Date:** 2026-03-10

## Findings

### Finding: Support leaders buy on personal value 2x more than business value
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Gong B2B buying research

> "Personal value has 2x more impact than business value for executive buyers"
> "Fear of messing up drives decisions more than FOMO"

Private fears (not stated):
1. "If this goes wrong, I own the failure" (blame risk)
2. "Will my team see this as threatening?" (team morale)
3. "Can I justify the cost to my CFO?" (budget risk)

**Implications:** Lead with risk mitigation, agent testimonials, and ROI proof—not features.

---

### Finding: Self-authored content outreach achieves 90% higher response
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Becc Holland methodology

> "Referencing prospect's own LinkedIn article or post achieves highest response rate"
> "Subject line example: 'Your article on agent burnout' (confuses everyone except them)"

Expected metrics:
- Open rate: 38-42% (vs 6% industry average)
- Reply rate: 12-18% (vs 1-2% industry average)
- Demo conversion: 25-30% of replies

**Implications:** Research each VP's LinkedIn content before outreach. Reference their specific posts.

---

### Finding: ROI calculators convert support leaders at 15-22%
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Fin AI ROI calculator analysis, B2B SaaS benchmarks

> "Fin AI ROI Calculator achieves 18-22% conversion from blog to calculator to demo"
> "Personalized output showing savings potential pre-qualifies high-intent leads"

Calculator inputs for support:
- Current tickets/month
- Average cost per employee
- Current automation rate

Outputs that convert:
- Estimated annual savings
- Agents freed up for complex work
- Payback period

**Implications:** Build support-specific ROI calculator as highest-priority conversion tool.

---

### Finding: Agent testimonials (not customer testimonials) address AI fear
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Sierra case studies, support leader interviews

The core fear: "Will this threaten my team or make them look bad?"

What works:
- Video testimonials from support agents (not managers)
- Quotes like: "I actually enjoy my job more now"
- Metric: "Agent satisfaction improved 22% in first 90 days"

What doesn't work:
- "AI works 24/7" (implies replacement)
- "Reduce headcount" (directly threatens)
- "Eliminate routine tickets" (sounds like job elimination)

**Implications:** Create case study content featuring agent voices, not just executives.

---

### Finding: Event-to-demo conversion is 25-35% for support leaders
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** B2B event marketing benchmarks

> "Support Driven Summit attendees convert at 25-35% post-event"
> "Roundtable discussions create warm relationships before outreach"

Effective event tactics:
- Host roundtable on "AI Implementation Best Practices"
- Offer "support health check" at booth
- Post-event follow-up within 24 hours referencing specific conversation

**Implications:** Prioritize Support Driven Summit and similar support-specific events.

---

### Finding: G2 reviews drive 8-15% demo conversion for support tools
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** G2 category analysis

> "G2 review → demo click: 8-12%"
> "G2 comparison page → demo: 15-20%"
> "Support leaders actively use G2 to justify purchases to leadership"

Priority actions:
- Target #1 Leader badge in "AI Customer Support Agents"
- Encourage customer reviews (5-star, emphasizing results)
- Create G2 comparison content ("Inkeep vs Zendesk vs Intercom")

**Implications:** Invest in G2 presence and competitive comparison content.

## Messaging Framework

**What NOT to say:**
- "AI agents work 24/7" → Implies replacement
- "Reduce support headcount" → Directly threatens
- "Eliminate routine tickets" → Sounds like job elimination
- "Advanced AI technology" → Feature, not outcome

**What TO say:**
- "Let your team focus on issues that matter"
- "Your agents can handle 50% more complex cases without burnout"
- "Reduce cost per ticket, not headcount"
- "AI handles the predictable; humans handle the novel"
