## Critical Issues Identified

### 🔴 Issue #1: Blog-to-Demo Funnel is Broken

**Evidence:**
- Blog content drives 3,000+ monthly visitors
- Only 1 out of 176 visitors from /blog/agent-frameworks-platforms-overview converted (0.57%)
- Top blog posts (/blog/technical-b2b-support-in-2026, /blog/composer-vs-swe) have **0% conversion**

**Root Cause:** Blog posts lack contextual CTAs that connect article topics to Inkeep's product. Generic "Get a Demo" in the nav is insufficient.

**Impact:** ~3,000 monthly visitors from blog content with near-zero conversion

### 🔴 Issue #2: Homepage-to-Demo Conversion is 0.20%

**Evidence:**
- 16,755 homepage visitors in last 90 days
- Only 33 clicked through directly to /demo

**Root Cause:** Homepage may not be effectively communicating the value prop or next step. Users may be browsing product pages but not reaching /demo.

**Impact:** The primary entry point has the lowest conversion rate of any page

### 🟠 Issue #3: /demo Page Drop-off is 96%

**Evidence:**
- 1,160 users reached /demo
- Only 44 submitted (3.79% conversion)

**Root Cause:** Demo page may lack:
- Social proof (testimonials, customer logos)
- Expectation setting ("30-minute call, see product live")
- Trust signals

### 🟠 Issue #4: High-Performing Pages Not Leveraged

**Evidence:**
- /case-studies has **23% conversion** to /demo but only 13 users saw it before /demo
- /blog/tag/enterprise-ai-agents has **8.22% conversion** but receives less traffic than individual posts

**Opportunity:** These pages should be more prominently linked from high-traffic blog content
