## Recommendations

### Immediate Actions (This Week)

#### 1. Audit Current Targeting Criteria in Unify

**Action:** Review and tighten ICP filters in UnifyGTM sequences

| Current Pattern | Recommendation |
|-----------------|----------------|
| Large enterprises (Booking, SymphonyAI, WeWork) | Focus on mid-market (50-500 employees) |
| No industry filter evident | Prioritize: Developer Tools, DevOps, FinTech |
| Generic outreach | Segment by use case |

**Why:** Won deal (Anvil) was 29 employees. Lost deals were large enterprises. Mid-market converts better.

#### 2. Add Pre-Qualification Gate

**Action:** Before creating opportunity in Unify, require:
- Confirmed budget discussion
- Identified decision maker
- Timeline under 90 days

**Why:** 38% disqualification rate means bad leads entering pipeline.

#### 3. Unstick Active Deals

| Deal | Days in Stage | Action |
|------|---------------|--------|
| Primer | 174 days in 5_Activation | Exec outreach or close |
| Cribl | 124 days in 1_Qualified | Re-qualify or disqualify |
| EDB | 98 days in 5_Activation | Set close date or exit |

### Sequence Optimization (This Month)

#### 4. Improve Demo Show Rate

**Current:** 56% of Direct leads get demos (good)

**To Improve:**
- Add calendar link in first email
- Implement Calendly/Chili Piper for instant booking
- Send reminder sequence 24h and 1h before demo
- Track no-show rate by sequence

#### 5. Segment Sequences by Company Size

| Segment | Employee Range | Messaging Focus |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|
| Startup | 1-50 | Speed to value, pricing |
| Mid-Market | 51-500 | ROI, integrations |
| Enterprise | 500+ | Security, compliance, support |

**Why:** One-size-fits-all messaging may work for booking demos but fails at conversion.

#### 6. Test New Personas

Current targeting appears to reach decision makers who book demos but don't buy.

**Test targeting:**
- Champions who can influence (Sr. Engineers, Tech Leads)
- End users who feel pain (Support Managers, Docs owners)
- Build bottom-up momentum before exec pitch

### Process Changes (This Quarter)

#### 7. Require Deal Amounts Earlier

**Problem:** 5 of 7 active Direct deals have no amount assigned.

**Action:** Require amount estimate at 1_Qualified stage or earlier.

**Why:** Can't forecast pipeline without amounts. Forces qualification conversation.

#### 8. Implement Lost Deal Analysis

For every Direct deal > $20K lost, document:
- Primary loss reason
- Competitor involved (if any)
- What would have changed outcome

**Priority lost deals to analyze:**
1. Booking.com ($50K)
2. SymphonyAI ($30K)
3. Leads.io ($30K)
4. Yotpo ($24K)

#### 9. Create Direct vs Indirect Playbooks

Direct (outbound) and Indirect (inbound) leads need different approaches:

| Stage | Direct Approach | Indirect Approach |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------|
| First Call | More discovery needed | They know the product |
| Demo | Customize heavily | Standard demo ok |
| Proposal | Build business case | Validate fit |
