### Finding: Gong hit $1M+ ARR in first year through founder-led sales
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** [Scribd Podcast Summary](https://www.scribd.com/podcast/417816852/1126-How-Gong-Hit-1m-in-ARR-in-1st-Year-Amit-Bendov-is-the-co-founder-and-CEO-of-Gong-io-the-1-conversation-intelligence-platform-for-sales-He)

> "Amit Bendov is the co-founder and CEO of Gong.io, the #1 conversation intelligence platform for sales. He achieved $1m+ in ARR in their first year."

**Implications:** Founder-led sales with 20+ years enterprise experience enabled rapid initial traction.

### Finding: Jasper scaled marketing spend from $50K to $1M/month in 30 days profitably

### Finding: Pendo founder refused to hire salespeople until $500K ARR
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** [Founderpath - Pendo](https://founderpath.com/resources/todd-olson-pendo-not-scaleable)

> "After two failed startups that couldn't find product-market fit, Todd Olson became obsessed with getting it right, spending a year doing founder-led sales and refusing to hire salespeople until he hit $500K ARR."

**Implications:** Founder-led sales until repeatable motion is validated prevents premature scaling.
