## Executive Summary

This report analyzes 8 outreach sequences targeting B2B support and CX personas, with 1,018 prospects who opened or clicked. Key findings:

1. **A/B Test Winner:** Fragmented (28.2% CTR) outperforms Manual Work (26.3% CTR)
2. **Best Subject Line:** "One platform to scale your technical support" (30.0% CTR at scale)
3. **Best CTA Link:** Platform decisions/build-vs-buy guide (31.9% CTR)
4. **Best Performing Sequence:** VP of Support (45.3% overall CTR)
5. **Highest Engaging Archetype:** Support Leaders (Manager+) at 51.9% click-to-open rate
6. **Top Engaging Title:** Client Service Manager (100% click-to-open), IT Support Manager (85.7%)

### Key Findings by Archetype

#### Support Leaders (Manager+) — Highest Engagement
- **Click-to-Open Rate:** 51.9%
- **Top Performers:** IT Support Manager (85.7%), Senior Technical Support Manager (70.0%), Technical Support Manager (56.1%)
- **Insight:** Technical support management titles show the highest intent. These are hands-on leaders experiencing the pain points daily.
- **Budget Authority:** VP level has full authority; Manager level influences decisions

#### Customer Success Leaders — Highest Volume
- **Click-to-Open Rate:** 39.0%
- **Top Performers:** Client Service Manager (100%), Client Experience Manager (63.0%), Manager of Customer Success (57.1%)
- **Insight:** "Client" prefix titles outperform generic "Customer Success" titles significantly
- **Budget Authority:** Growing authority but often needs VP approval for larger purchases

#### VP of Customer Experience — Decision Makers
- **Click-to-Open Rate:** 31.0%
- **Top Performers:** Customer Experience Manager (61.1%), VP Customer Experience (33.3%)
- **Insight:** Lower click rate but these are budget holders. Quality over quantity.
- **Budget Authority:** Full authority for CX tools

### Recommendations by Role Archetype

| Archetype | Messaging Approach | CTA Strategy |
|-----------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Support Leaders (Manager+)** | Lead with T2/T3 pain points, time-to-reproduce, manual scavenger hunt | ROI calculator, 5-minute demo, template offer |
| **Customer Success Leaders** | Focus on burnout, scaling personalized engagement, TTV | Free template, "See how [company] improved TTV by X%" |
| **VP of Customer Experience** | Prove ROI to leadership, cross-functional alignment, NRR | Executive summary, specific date/time CTA |
| **Support/CX Ops Leaders** | Tool consolidation, integration challenges, automation ROI | Technical deep-dive, API documentation, sandbox access |

### Title Patterns That Signal High Intent

1. **"Client" prefix** (Client Experience Manager, Client Service Manager) — 70%+ click rates
2. **"Technical" + "Manager"** (Technical Support Manager) — 56% click rate
3. **"Senior" + Support titles** — indicates decision influence
4. **"IT Support"** titles — 85%+ click rates, strong technical pain point resonance

### Title Patterns That Signal Lower Intent

1. **"VP of Customer Experience"** — opens but lower click rate (20%)
2. **"Head of Customer Experience"** — 0% click rate (8 opens, 0 clicks)
3. **"Technical Support Lead"** — 0% click rate (9 opens, 0 clicks)
4. **Generic "Enterprise Customer Success Manager"** — 33% vs 63% for Client Experience

### Actionable Insights

1. **Prioritize Support Leaders (Manager+)** — 51.9% click-to-open rate indicates strong resonance with current messaging
2. **Target "Client Experience" titles specifically** — outperform generic CS titles by 20+ percentage points
3. **Adjust VP of CX messaging** — high opens but low clicks suggest subject lines work but content/CTA needs refinement
4. **Create separate sequences for IT Support personas** — 85%+ engagement suggests distinct pain points worth addressing
5. **Deprioritize "Head of" titles** — lower engagement suggests these roles may not be ideal ICP
