## Findings

### Finding: Founder-CEOs prioritize revenue metrics above all
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Multiple sources confirm MRR, ARR, CAC, NRR, and churn as primary metrics

**Implications:** Cold email messaging must connect to revenue impact, not features

---

### Finding: CTOs face AI adoption pressure as top priority
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** McKinsey, CTO Magazine, and Softtek all cite AI integration and GenAI adoption as critical 2025 priorities

**Implications:** AI-related tools have strong entry point, but must show practical value delivery

---

### Finding: VPs of Engineering care most about developer productivity
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** McKinsey 2024 data shows developers spend only 32% of time writing code; DORA metrics are primary KPIs

**Implications:** Productivity claims must be specific and measurable

---

### Finding: Average B2B purchase involves 13 stakeholders
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Forrester 2024 research, cited by multiple sources

**Implications:** Multi-threading strategy is essential; single-point outreach insufficient

---

### Finding: 41% of B2B buyers have preferred vendor before formal evaluation
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Gartner SaaS Buying Experience research

**Implications:** Brand awareness and early-stage engagement critical

---

### Finding: Targeting 10+ people drops reply rates from 7.8% to 3.8%
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Belkins cold email response rate study

**Implications:** Narrow targeting (1-3 people) outperforms spray-and-pray
