**Evidence:** ProductLed State of B2B SaaS 2025 report

**Implications:** Multi-touch, multi-channel strategies essential

### Finding: 60% of B2B buyers prefer self-led purchasing
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Reprise PLG research

### Finding: 42% of companies abandoned most AI initiatives in 2025
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED

## Content Consumption Patterns

### VP/Head of Growth
- **Podcasts:** SaaStr Podcast, The Growth Hub (Advance B2B)
- **Communities:** Reforge, FINITE, SaaStock, B2B Entrepreneurs (17k+ members)
- **Format preference:** Quick educational content, micro demos, customer stories

### Growth Manager
- **Communities:** Reforge courses, B2B Entrepreneurs, FINITE
- **Format preference:** Practical frameworks, case studies from similar-stage companies
- **Tools:** Mixpanel, Amplitude, Statsig, Pendo, Chameleon

### AI Transformation Leader
- **Newsletters:** The AI Optimist, Ben's Bites (140k+ subscribers), TAAFT (1.7M subscribers)
- **Reports:** McKinsey AI research, Gartner AI reports, MIT CISR
- **Format preference:** Executive summaries, ROI frameworks, governance frameworks

### Content Creator (Recruiting)
- **Podcasts:** Employer Branding Unfiltered, The Employer Branding Podcast
- **Newsletters:** Recruiting Brainfood
- **Format preference:** Practical how-to, case studies, templates
