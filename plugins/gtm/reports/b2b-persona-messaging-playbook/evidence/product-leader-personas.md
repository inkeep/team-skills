## Findings

### Finding: Managing competing stakeholder priorities is #1 PM challenge
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Ant Murphy 2024 survey, The Top Voices report both cite stakeholder management as top challenge

**Implications:** Messaging that addresses cross-functional alignment resonates

---

### Finding: 83% of B2B buyers prefer self-service during discovery
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** The Insight Collective B2B buyer behavior study

**Implications:** Provide low-friction ways to evaluate (trials, demos, docs) before calls

---

### Finding: 57% expect positive ROI within 3 months
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** The Insight Collective research on B2B tech buyers

**Implications:** Time-to-value messaging critical; avoid long implementation timelines

---

### Finding: Interest-based CTAs achieve 30% response rate vs 15% for direct booking
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Smartlead cold email CTA research

**Implications:** "Would it be helpful to see..." outperforms "Book a demo"

---

### Finding: Platform PMs struggle to quantify infrastructure impact
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Ant Murphy's analysis of platform PM challenges; Medium article by Lambros Charissis

**Implications:** Help them articulate business value of platform improvements

---

### Finding: Directors have budget authority; ICs influence but rarely decide
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Multiple sources confirm budget authority hierarchy

**Implications:** Adjust CTA and messaging based on seniority level

## Content Consumption Patterns

### Director/Head of Product
- **Newsletters:** Lenny's Newsletter (300k+ subscribers), Mind the Product (Prioritised)
- **Podcasts:** Lenny's Podcast, The Product Experience
- **Communities:** Mind the Product Slack (60k+ members), Product School Slack (30k members)

### Senior PM/GPM
- **Communities:** Launch Awesome Slack, Creators of Products Slack (500+ active weekly)
- **Format preference:** Deep-dive case studies, prioritization frameworks

### Mid-level/Junior PM
- **Communities:** Product Tribes Discord (10k+ members), FoundIt Discord
- **Format preference:** Templates, career advice, tactical how-tos

### Technical/Platform PM
- **Communities:** Platform Engineering community, technical Slack channels
- **Format preference:** Technical architecture case studies, API documentation

## Tool Evaluation Criteria by Level

| Level | Primary Evaluation Criteria |
|-------|---------------------------|
| Director/Head | Security (81% factor breach history), strategic alignment, vendor support |
| Senior PM/GPM | Centralization, prioritization features, integrations |
| Mid-level PM | Ease of use, free tier availability, quick time-to-value |
| Junior PM | Learning curve, templates, peer adoption |
| Technical PM | API quality, integration depth, scalability |
