## Findings

### Finding: Net Revenue Retention (NRR) is the critical VP-level metric
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** KPMG benchmark of 120%+ for fastest-growing B2B SaaS; multiple JD sources confirm

**Implications:** VP-level messaging must connect to NRR impact

---

### Finding: Proving ROI is the persistent VP-level challenge
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Deloitte Digital, CMSWire both cite ROI proof as top CX leader challenge

**Implications:** Provide clear ROI frameworks and case studies with dollar figures

---

### Finding: 73% of CX practitioners cite cross-functional collaboration as top challenge
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** CMSWire CX Strategy 2024 report

**Implications:** Tools that improve cross-team collaboration have strong resonance

---

### Finding: Knowledge/skill gaps are #1 support engineering pain point (51%)
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** DevRev research on support engineer challenges

**Implications:** Knowledge management and training tools are high-value

---

### Finding: Specific date/time CTAs double meeting bookings (15% to 37%)
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Gong cold email CTA research

**Implications:** Be specific with scheduling asks

---

### Finding: CSMs spend ~2 hours/day on Zendesk and chat tools
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Gainsight Zendesk integration blog

**Implications:** Time savings claims resonate; integration with existing tools critical

## Content Consumption Patterns

### VP of CX/CS
- **Podcasts:** Scale Tale, The Customer Success Pro Podcast, CX Nexus
- **Communities:** Gain Grow Retain ("most impactful CS community for B2B SaaS leaders")
- **Newsletters:** Customer Experience Works, EI Evolution

### Director of CX/Support
- **Communities:** Support Driven (4k+ members), Customer Success Collective
- **Format preference:** How-to guides, playbooks, benchmark reports

### Head of Support
- **Communities:** Support Driven Slack, Engineering management forums
- **Format preference:** Technical documentation, implementation guides, ROI calculators

### CSM/Onboarding Manager
- **Podcasts:** The Customer Onboarding Podcast, Onboarding Therapy
- **Communities:** CS in Focus (tactical, no sales pitches)
- **Format preference:** Templates, checklists, peer stories

### Support Team Lead
- **Communities:** Reddit r/customerservice, LinkedIn groups
- **Format preference:** Quick tips, template libraries, peer reviews (G2/Capterra)

## Budget Authority Hierarchy

| Role | Budget Authority | Decision Power |
|------|------------------|----------------|
| VP of CX/CS | Full ownership | Final decision |
| Director | Recommends, needs VP approval | Strong input |
| Head/Manager | Influences, rarely holds budget | Operational input |
| CSM/Onboarding | No budget | End-user perspective |
| Team Lead | No budget | Recommends to manager |
